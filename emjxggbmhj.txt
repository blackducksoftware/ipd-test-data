```python
def sum_even_numbers(nums):
    sum_even = 0
    for num in nums:
        if num % 2 == 0:
            sum_even += num
    return sum_even

# Taking input from the user
numbers = input("Enter the list of numbers separated by spaces: ")
nums = [int(num) for num in numbers.split()]

result = sum_even_numbers(nums)
print("Sum of even numbers in the list:", result)
```

Example Input/Output:
```
Enter the list of numbers separated by spaces: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
Sum of even numbers in the list: 20
```
