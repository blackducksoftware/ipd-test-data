```python
def sum_of_evens(numbers):
    even_sum = 0
    for num in numbers:
        if num % 2 == 0:
            even_sum += num
    return even_sum

# Taking input from the user
numbers = input("Enter a list of numbers separated by space: ")
numbers = list(map(int, numbers.split()))

result = sum_of_evens(numbers)
print("Sum of even numbers in the list:", result)
```

Example Input/Output:
```
Enter a list of numbers separated by space: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9
Sum of even numbers in the list: 20
```
